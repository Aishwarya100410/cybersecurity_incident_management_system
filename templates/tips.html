Top Strategies to Stay Cyber Safe

  • Recognize and Report Incidents: Identify suspicious activities and report them promptly using the designated channels.
  • Follow Security Policies: Adhere to company policies, access control measures, and use authorized devices only.
  • Practice Good Cyber Hygiene: Use strong, unique passwords, update software regularly, and back up important data.
  • Stay Informed and Trained: Participate in regular cybersecurity training sessions and stay aware of phishing attacks.
  • Use Secure Communication: Encrypt sensitive data and avoid using public Wi-Fi for work without a VPN.
  • Report Lost or Stolen Devices Immediately: Inform the IT or security team if a device is lost or stolen to secure your data.
  • Practice Safe Browsing: Avoid suspicious websites and use browser security features like pop-up blockers.
  • Be Cautious with External Devices: Scan USB drives with antivirus software before use and limit personal devices for work.
  • Follow Incident Response Guidance: Cooperate with the incident response team and follow their instructions during an incident.
  • Maintain Physical Security: Lock your computer when away from your desk and secure sensitive documents.
